Vote 1

Citizenship & Immigration Control

The Directorate of Citizenship and Immigration Control (CIC) is responsible for enforcing immigration laws and regulations, regulating the entry, stay and exit of persons from Uganda, and issuing travel documents to Ugandan citizens and eligible foreign nationals.

The Directorate plays a critical role in national security by ensuring that only eligible individuals enter and remain in Uganda, and that Ugandan citizens travelling abroad are properly documented.

Vote 2

Government Analytical Laboratory

The Government Analytical Laboratory (GAL) is Uganda's premier public sector forensic and analytical science facility. It provides scientific and technical services to government agencies, law enforcement, the judiciary, and the general public.

GAL's scope includes forensic chemistry, DNA analysis, ballistics, toxicology, document examination, and environmental analysis — all critical for the justice system and public health.
