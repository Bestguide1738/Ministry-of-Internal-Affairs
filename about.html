About the Ministry

The Ministry of Internal Affairs is a Government of Uganda Ministry mandated to ensure internal security, maintain law and order, promote peace and stability, and ensure the registration and identification of all citizens of Uganda. The Ministry was established under the Constitution of Uganda and continues to serve as one of the pillars of good governance in the country.

The Ministry works in close coordination with the Uganda Police Force, the Directorate of Citizenship and Immigration Control, the National Identification and Registration Authority (NIRA), and other affiliated bodies to deliver its mandate effectively.

Mission Statement

To provide effective and efficient services that promote internal security, law and order, peace and stability, citizen identification and protection in Uganda through the development and implementation of sound policies, laws and regulations.

Vision

A peaceful, secure and stable Uganda where every citizen is identified and protected.

Ministry Objectives

🛡️ Internal Security

Guarantee Uganda's internal security through proactive policies and enforcement mechanisms.

⚖️ Law & Order

Ensure the rule of law is upheld across all sectors and regions of the country.

🕊️ Peace & Stability

Promote peace and stability through dialogue, amnesty and community engagement.

🪪 Citizen Identification

Ensure the identification, protection and preservation of all Ugandan citizens.

🔬 Forensic & Analytical

Provide scientific support to justice through the Government Analytical Laboratory.

🌍 Immigration Control

Regulate immigration and citizenship to protect Uganda's national interests.

Leadership

The Ministry is headed by the Minister of Internal Affairs, who is a Cabinet Minister appointed by the President of Uganda. The Ministry is supported by the Permanent Secretary, who provides administrative leadership, and several Directors who oversee the various departments and directorates.

The current Minister of Internal Affairs is Gen. Kahinda Otafire, a distinguished senior government official with extensive experience in public service and national security.

Organogram

The Ministry is structured into several departments and directorates, each responsible for a specific function. These include:

  • Office of the Minister
  • Office of the Permanent Secretary
  • Directorate of Citizenship and Immigration Control
  • Government Analytical Laboratory
  • Amnesty Commission
  • Department of Community Service
  • Government Security Office
  • National Focal Point
  • Counter-Trafficking in Persons Unit
  • Finance & Administration Department
  • Planning and Policy Analysis Department
